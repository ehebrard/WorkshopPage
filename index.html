Overview

This workshop will be held on May 29 2012, in conjunction with CP-AI-OR, and aims to support exchanges between OR, CP and SAT communities on the topic of search heuristics and strategies. Moreover, beyond usual optimisation objectives, this workshop also aims at being a forum on more complex criteria and their formulations, such as, but not limited to, solution robustness and fault tolerance, or cost balancing and fairness objectives, or solution sampling and enumeration.

Scope

We welcome articles on emergent and promising generic heuristics and descriptions of dedicated search strategies for a particular application as well as studies and models for less conventional objectives. The scope of this workshop includes, but is not restricted to:
  • Generic heuristics;
  • Dedicated search strategies;
  • Adaptation of heuristics between different frameworks;
  • Solution robustness and fault tolerance;
  • Cost balancing and fairness;
  • Solutions sampling, diversity;
  • Solutions regularity and stability
